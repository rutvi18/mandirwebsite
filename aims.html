Aims & Object


Shri Balaji Maharaj Seva Trust is constituted with the following aims and object.
1. To execute and extension of social services of Mandir Shri Brajdham Balaji Maharaj; Avairni.
2. To enrich the Dharmic Anusthan, social and Educational activities, cultural heritage etc.
3. To organize various Dharmik programs and special Anusthan for welfare of society.
4. To organize various medical camp - eye-camp. etc. for welfare of poor section of society.
5. To support and extend the social services for Girls-Education and oldage-people
Besides these objectives, trust will manage various functions to achieve the aims and objects like - donations and extention of social services for poor section of society.
