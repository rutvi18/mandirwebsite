Introduction
The “SHRI BALAJI MAHARAJ SEVA TRUST” was constituted by founder chairman of SBMS Trust Dr.Ram Niwas Sharma established in the spiritual will of his father (Founder Mahantjee of Shri Brajdham Balaji Maharaj Mandir, Avairni). Late Shri Jagannath Pd. Jee to serve the society for upliftment.
The Prakatya of Divya Swaroop of Shri Brajdham Balaji Maharaj was a result of continuous devotion and non-stopage Bhakti Sadhana of Founder Mahant Shri Jagannath Prasadji.
The Divya Darshan and blessing of Shri Ram bhakt Hanumant Lal bless the people to get rid of their various problems and get benefited by Ashirvad of Balaji Maharaj.
SHRI BRAJDHAM BALAJI MAHARAJ MANDIR
The mandir of Shri Braj Dham Balaji Maharaj is located at 2 km from the famous temple of Shri Brajdham Aanandkand Thakur Shri Dauji Maharaj at Baldeo-Jugasana road in village named Avairni (Mathura).
The mandir is 15 km from Twonship chauraha of Mathura (NH-2) on Mathura-Sadabad road.
In holi-land of Braj Raj Shri DAuji Maharaj and Revati Maiya; Seth Karkalli established a KARKALLESHWAR MAHADEV TEMPLE in 1875 a village named Avairni(near to Dauji at 2 km distance).
As a result of fruitful spiritual will and extreme devotion of Mahant Shri Jagannath Prasadji the entire world got chance to get blessing showers of SHANKAR suban RUDRA-AVATAR Shri Balaji Maharaj in 1055 in Karkalleshwar Mahadev temple of Avairni.
In Garv-Grah of temple , beside Divya Swaroop of RamDarbar, PretRaj Sarkar, Bhairav baba, Maa Saraswati, Maa Durgaji; the pindi of Pahad wale baba and Shri Kakajee Maharaj are available along wth Balaji Maharaj.
Sheshavatar DAuji Maharaj and Revati Maiya, Radha-Krishna, Ganesh-Laxmi and Guruji Maharaj Shri Jagannath Pd. Mahantjee shower the blessings to people for wellfare of society.
Gaulokvasi Mahantji Maharaj
Founder Mahant Late Shri Jagannath Prasad Mahantjee was born in a Brahmin family of village Akhaigarh distt-Bharatpur of Rajasthan on Paush sudi Ekadasi of Vikramisambat 1975 (Dec. 1918). The eldest son of Pt Ghoodmal & Gulkandi devi, named JAGAN was very spiritual having a great Bhakti of Lord Hanumanjee since his childhood.
Shri Jagannath Pd. Mahantjee was having special blessing of Shri Pret Raj Sarkar with the shower blessing of Rambhakt Hanymant Lal and Pret Raj Sarkar, Mahant Shri Jagannath Prasad organized various regular Anusthan to serve the people having various types of problems. As a result of which many people got blessed to achieve their respective Manoraths.
Shri Jagannath Prasad Jee served a lot with spiritual blessing of Ram bhakt Hanuman and went to Gaulok Dham on 4th Jyesth vadi Vikram Sambat 2060 (19th May 2003) having his soul with Shri Brajdham Balaji Maharaj.
SHRI ANJANI-DHAM SATSANG-BHAWAN
With a lots of blessing of Shri Braj-Dham Balaji Maharaj & spiritual inspiration of Shri Guruji Maharaj Pt. Jagannath Prasadji; Sons of Lala Chunnilal; resident of AGRA, have developed Shri Anjanidham Satsang Bhawan and Savitri Guest House in the memories of their Poorvaj-Pitars with the spiritual inspiration of Late mother Smt.Santri Devi.
In satsang bhawan (well established by family memers of Late Chunnilal and Savitri devi) Ramayan Path of Shri Ram Charit Manas and Havan at each Purnima is organized regularily.
ANJANI DHAM SATSANG BHAWAN Provides the sufficient infrastructural facilities for various spiritual programmes like Bhagwat Katha, Shri Ram Katha, Ekadasi udhyapan Sankeertanes.
Savitri Guest House have many rooms with all facilities for Guests.
